# Gigmark for Financial Partners

## What is Gigmark?

Gigmark is a **proof-of-work identity platform for Africa's informal workforce**. It transforms completed gigs into verified economic identity, allowing workers to build trust and unlock financial opportunities without CVs or credit history.

---

## The Opportunity for Partners

**45M+ informal workers** across Africa operate outside formal systems. They lack:
- Verified income history
- Credit records
- Employment references
- Digital identity proof

**Partners** (banks, lenders, insurers, fintech) have been unable to serve this market reliably. Gigmark solves this by creating **bankable signals from work itself**.

---

## How it Works

### 1. **Worker Onboarding** (AI-driven, 2-3 min)
- Voice or text-based signup
- Skills + work type captured
- Financial goal identified upfront

### 2. **Gig Completion** (Real work)
- Matched to employers
- Work completed and verified
- Payment processed (via Squad)

### 3. **Financial Twin** (Live scoring)
- Trust score updated live
- Financial readiness score derived
- Bank offers surfaced in real-time

### 4. **Partner Handoff** (You reach out)
- Gigmark flags eligible workers
- Partner API receives profile + financial signals
- Partner can issue credit, savings, insurance

## FAQs

**Q: How do you verify workers?**  
A: Through gig completion history, employer ratings, and payment consistency. No KYC initially; behavioral signals build trust.

**Q: What if a worker defaults?**  
A: Gigmark trust score tanks, employer is protected via escrow, and lender can assess default risk like any other portfolio.

**Q: Can we white-label this?**  
A: Yes. Partner can rebrand worker flows and API endpoints.

**Q: What about compliance?**  
A: We're building with legal counsel. Each partner responsible for their own lending/insurance regulatory compliance.
