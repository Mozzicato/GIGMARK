# Gigmark PRD

## "Proof-of-Work Identity for Africa's Informal Workforce"

---

# 1. Product Overview

## Product Name

**Gigmark**

## One-Line Description

Gigmark transforms completed real-world gigs into verified economic identity, allowing informal workers to build trust, reputation, and financial access without traditional CVs or credit history.

## Vision

To become the trust infrastructure for Africa's informal economy.

## Mission

Enable millions of informal workers and unemployed youth to prove their value through work, not paperwork.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Millions of skilled African youths operate outside formal economic systems because they lack:

* CVs
* verified job history
* bank credit records
* professional references
* trusted digital identity

As a result:

* employers cannot verify trust,
* lenders cannot assess risk,
* and workers remain economically invisible.

Current platforms focus on job listings, not portable economic reputation.

---

# 3. Solution

Gigmark creates a **Proof-of-Work Identity**.

Every completed gig:

* becomes verified work history,
* updates a live trust score,
* contributes to an AI-generated economic profile,
* and unlocks financial opportunities.

Instead of:

> "Show us your CV"

Workers show:

> "Here is verified proof of my work."

---

# 4. Core Innovation

## A. Invisible CV

Users never manually build resumes.

Gigmark automatically generates:

* work history
* skill confidence
* reliability metrics
* professional summaries

using behavioral data.

---

## B. Proof-of-Work Identity

Every completed task becomes:

* a verifiable economic record,
* permanently attached to the user profile.

This acts as:

* reputation,
* employability proof,
* and financial credibility.

---

## C. AI Economic Twin

Gigmark creates a predictive economic model for users using:

* gig completion patterns
* payment consistency
* communication quality
* dispute frequency
* customer sentiment
* earnings growth

The system predicts:

* reliability,
* earning potential,
* loan eligibility,
* and suitable opportunities.

---

## D. Voice AI Accessibility

Users can:

* onboard,
* post gigs,
* and communicate

through voice notes in local languages.

AI converts voice into structured platform actions.

# 6. User Journey

## Worker Flow

1. User joins with phone number
2. AI voice onboarding extracts skills
3. User receives first micro-gig
4. Gig completed and verified
5. Squad processes payment
6. Trust score updates live
7. Proof-of-work profile grows
8. Financial products unlock

---

## Employer Flow

1. Employer posts gig
2. AI recommends workers
3. Employer selects worker
4. Funds held in escrow
5. Work delivered
6. Payment released via Squad
7. Worker reputation updated
