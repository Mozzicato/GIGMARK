# Gigmark Pitch Script

## 90-second version

"Millions of African workers do real work every day, but the economy still cannot see them properly.

They do not have polished CVs.
They do not have formal credit history.
They often do not have the kind of documentation banks or employers expect.

So even when they are skilled and reliable, they remain economically invisible.

Gigmark changes that.

We turn completed gigs into verified proof-of-work identity.

A worker can onboard through a simple AI conversation in text or voice.
They do not need to write a professional resume.
As they complete jobs, Gigmark builds a live trust profile using ratings, proof-of-work records, payouts, and work history.

For employers, this means safer hiring.
They can see who is trustworthy, fund jobs through escrow, and choose workers with stronger proof.

For workers, this means more than getting hired once.
It means every completed gig becomes a step toward savings, credit, and financial access.

And this is where Squad matters.
Squad powers the money movement behind Gigmark, from hosted checkout to transfer-based funding through dynamic virtual accounts, payment verification, and escrow confidence.

So the big idea is this:
work becomes proof,
proof becomes identity,
and identity unlocks opportunity.

That is Gigmark." 

## 2-minute judged demo intro

"We are building Gigmark, a proof-of-work identity platform for Africa's informal workforce.

Today, millions of workers are skilled and active, but still invisible to formal systems because they do not have CVs, verified job history, or lender-readable financial records.

Gigmark solves that by turning completed work itself into an identity asset.

The worker onboards quickly through AI.
The employer hires from a trust-aware dashboard.
Payments are protected through Squad-powered escrow and transfer rails.
And every completed job improves the worker's trust and financial readiness profile.

So instead of asking, 'Where is your CV?'
Gigmark lets the worker say, 'Here is proof of my work.'

Let me show you how that works end to end." 

## Close

"Gigmark is not just helping people find gigs.
It is building the trust infrastructure that allows informal work to become visible, verifiable, and financeable."
